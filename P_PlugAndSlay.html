Plug & Slay

Plug in, shoot your friends. Get upgrades running down the conveyor belt, become OP, destroy your friends.

Plug & Slay is a local multiplayer game I developed in a team of 5, in which I was the lead programmer and designer.

Working with non programmers

I was in charge of every system that other team members who weren't programmers would interact with. Meaning I made things such as the character selection screen, which the artists needed to be able to use. Which is why I made it artist friendly by making a simple, but really useful inspector interface for adding new characters.
