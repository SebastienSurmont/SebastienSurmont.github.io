Plug n' Slay

client game team of 5

A local multiplayer game for 2-4 players, with a variety of maps and game modes.
You can only shoot when you're plugged into a socket, which has limited range & ammo.

Goal

Turn a client's prototype into a game to test out the idea, with certain restriction. Such as having to keep the game strategic, while also being fun and chaotic.



My role

I took the role of lead developer & technical designer. Because of my diverse background I took a very central role between the client, the artists, and the other developers.
Explaining design ideas to both artists & programmers, as well as helping with communication between them.
