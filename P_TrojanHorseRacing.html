Background

Along with 4 friends I participated in a 3 day, in person, game jam called Xbox Gamecamp. We had to make a game in 3 days to create a game based on 1 of 3 categories.
We won in our category (minesweeper) as well as best overall. I took the role of lead developer & technical designer.

Simplicity as a strength

Before the gamejam I gave the team 1 goal, finish the game in the first day and a half. Then we could focus the rest of our time on purely polishing the game. This meant that my main focus as developer wasn't to cram in features, but to make the basic features, such as exploding windows & movement, feel flawless. It's because of our scaled down version of making a full game, that we were able to win the competition.
